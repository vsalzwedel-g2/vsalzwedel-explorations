Design Notes

  • Layout: Horizontal flex (avatar | content). Content stacks vertically (title, seller, rating).
  • Avatar sizes: xxs=24px, xs=48px, sm=64px, md=80px, lg=104px, xl=144px
  • Default: :md (80px avatar, 16px title, 14px seller/rating)
  • Title sizes: xxs/xs/sm→14px, md→16px, lg→20px, xl→24px
  • Gaps: xxs/xs=4px, sm=8px, md=12px, lg=16px, xl=20px
  • Star rating: Uses inline star_rating component (:sm for most, :lg for lg/xl sizes)
  • Conditional elements:
    • xxs: Title only (no seller, no rating)
    • xs: Title + rating (no seller)
    • sm and above: Title + seller + rating
  • Use cases:
    • xxs: Ultra-compact lists
    • xs: Dense tables, chips
    • sm: Compact cards
    • md: Product cards, search results (DEFAULT)
    • lg: Category pages, expanded cards
    • xl: Product headers, hero sections
