Accessibility Notes

  • Semantic Meaning: Always pair color with text—never rely on color alone
  • ARIA Labels: Use aria-label or role="status" for dynamic badges
  • Contrast: All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards
  • Non-interactive: Badges are display-only, not clickable
