Usage Guidelines

  • Size Selection: Use xs for compact lists, sm for sidebar navigation, md for featured products
  • Rating Display: Only md size includes star rating - smaller sizes focus on product name
  • Interactivity: Always use as clickable link (not button) to product page
  • Avatar: Use product logo or brand icon - ensure sufficient contrast on white background
  • Title: Truncate long product names with ellipsis to maintain chip width

Accessibility Notes

  • Semantics: Use <a> tag with href for navigation
  • Alt Text: Provide descriptive alt text for product avatar images
  • Focus Indicators: Purple focus ring is visible and meets WCAG standards
  • Touch Targets: Ensure sufficient size for mobile taps (min 44x44px)
  • Rating Context: Include review count to provide context beyond just star rating
