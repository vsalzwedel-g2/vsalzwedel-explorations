Accessibility Notes

  • Alt Text: Always provide descriptive alt text for avatar images
  • ARIA Labels: Use aria-label for notification badges to convey count
  • Color Independence: Initials are readable on purple background (WCAG AA)
  • Focus States: If avatar is interactive (clickable), add visible focus ring
