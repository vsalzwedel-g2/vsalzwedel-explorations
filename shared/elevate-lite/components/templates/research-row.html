Specifications

  • Container: flex row, items-center, gap-4 (16px), px-5 py-4 (20px horizontal, 16px vertical)
  • Border: border-light, rounded-md (6px)
  • Hover: shadow-2, border-medium
  • Label: text-sm (14px), font-semibold, text-subtle, whitespace-nowrap
  • Title: text-sm (14px), font-semibold, text-default, flex-1 (grows to fill space)
  • Tag chips: h-6 (24px), flex gap-2 container, single-select pattern
  • Timestamp: text-2xs (11px), text-nonessential, whitespace-nowrap
  • Tag click: preventDefault and stopPropagation to allow filtering without navigating
  • List spacing: gap-2 (8px) between rows
