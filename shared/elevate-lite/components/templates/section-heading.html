Specifications

  • Label text: text-lg (18px), font-bold (700), text-default (#201f23)
  • Count text: text-lg (18px), font-normal (400), text-subtle (#57534E)
  • Usage: Precedes content sections like grids or lists
  • Spacing: Typically mb-4 (16px) below the heading
